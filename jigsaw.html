Baseball 101 - Jigsaw Activity

In this activity you will work in a group of three students. You will be given a number 1-3. Research your topic to become an expert on it.

Directions:

  1. Get together with your pre-assigned group of three other students.
  2. Each member of the group will be assigned a number from 1 to 3. Look at the table below to see what basic baseball skill you correspond with.
  3. Use the 3 links provided for your topic so you can research it.
  4. Join a new group with students that have experted the same component of baseball as you did. Discuss your topic and answer the questions.
  5. Finally, return to your group. Each student will get a chance to teach the group about their component of baseball that they researched.
  6. Lastly, wait for your turn to present to the class.
