Netiquette for baseball

Stay in shape during off-season

It is important to be in shape during the offseason to maintain endurance and stamin for training and next season.

No use of drugs

The use of drugs or smoking/juuling can put a setback on your performance, at the same time harming you.

Keep up with education

Education is important, you can't become a pro if you didn't go to college. Having bad grades will result in being kicked off the team.

Be on time to games

Being on time shows your coach that you care and also could build a positive reputation for yourself.
