Web Accessibility

Summary of Web Accessibility

Web accessibility is ensuring there are no barriers that prevent interaction with, or access to, websites on the Internet by people with physical disabilities, situational disabilities as in poor location resulting in slow speed, and socio-economic restrictions on someone's bandwidth and internet speed.

Color Blindness

Color blindness is when there is a problem with the pigment in certain eye areas, affecting the view of color. It isn't fatal, but it permanently affects the way you see color. It's common, 1 million cases a year which are mostly found in men. There are treatments coming out and medication. There has also been a type of glasses designed to allow color blind people see color.

Here are some types of color blindness:

  • Red-Green; Only shown in men, excusing the few cases where a woman will be affected
  • L-Cone Defect: effects 2.45% of men and .04% of women
  • M-Cone Defect: effects 6.1% of men and .36% of women
  • S-Cone Defect: .011% of men
